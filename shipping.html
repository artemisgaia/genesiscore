Shipping Policy

Genesis Core shipping is built for predictable fulfillment, clear carrier handoff, and controlled destination coverage.

Effective date: February 9, 2026

Last reviewed: February 9, 2026

Processing

Order Handling Standards

  • Orders are generally processed within 2 to 5 business days before carrier pickup.
  • Business days exclude weekends and carrier-observed holidays.
  • Transit windows are estimates only and do not constitute guaranteed delivery dates.

Dispatch

Tracking and Risk Transfer

  • Tracking details are issued after carrier acceptance scan.
  • Risk of loss and title transfer to customer at carrier handoff, where allowed by applicable law.
  • Genesis Core is not liable for delay caused by carrier backlog, weather, or customs authorities.

Where We Ship

United States

  • Lower 48 states, Alaska, and Hawaii are supported.
  • Availability for U.S. territories may vary by carrier lane and regulatory constraints.

Customs and Duties

International Import Responsibility

  • Duties, VAT, and import handling fees are customer responsibility unless listed at checkout.
  • Customer must comply with destination-country supplement regulations before ordering.
  • Rejected, refused, or abandoned customs shipments may incur return and handling deductions.

Legal Conditions

Limitations and Force Majeure

  • Delivery commitments are suspended for force majeure events, including strikes, disasters, and border controls.
  • Genesis Core may cancel shipment to destinations with legal or operational risk without prior notice.
  • Nothing in this policy limits mandatory consumer protections under applicable local law.
