# Genesis Core Non-Product AI Image Prompts

Use these prompts for all brand and UX imagery except product packshots.  
Brand direction: clinical, precise, premium, calm, engineered.

## Global style controls
- Aspect ratio variants needed: `16:9`, `3:2`, `1:1`, `4:5`.
- Lighting: soft directional studio light, controlled highlights, no blown whites.
- Palette: slate navy, steel gray, clean white, subtle copper accent.
- Texture: brushed aluminum, frosted glass, matte ceramic, clean lab surfaces.
- Avoid: pills in hand, before/after body claims, medical diagnosis visuals, hype fitness clich√©s.
- Composition: generous negative space for text overlays.
