Policy

Returns, Refunds, and Replacements

Supplements are ingestible products. For safety and quality control reasons, claims are accepted only for verified fulfillment or product-quality failures.

Effective date: February 9, 2026

Last reviewed: February 9, 2026

Eligible Claims

Cases We Review

  • Product received damaged in transit.
  • Defect or integrity issue confirmed on arrival.
  • Incorrect product shipped.
  • Missing item from a verified shipment.

Non-Returnable Cases

Cases Excluded by Policy

  • Opened or used products returned for taste, preference, or buyer remorse.
  • Claims submitted outside the required reporting period.
  • Unauthorized return shipments sent without prior approval.
  • Customs refusal or non-payment of import fees by the customer.

Claim Window

7-Day Reporting Requirement

Claims must be submitted within 7 calendar days from carrier-marked delivery date. Claims submitted after this period may be denied.

Claim Evidence

Required Documentation

  • Order number and order email.
  • Clear issue description.
  • Photos of outer box, shipping label, and affected product.

Resolution Standard

Replacement or Refund

  • Approved claims are resolved by replacement or refund, at Genesis Core's election.
  • Refunds are issued to original payment method only.
  • Bank and card posting timelines are outside Genesis Core control.

Legal Conditions

Chargebacks, Fraud, and Jurisdiction

  • Fraudulent claims, altered evidence, or abusive chargebacks may lead to account suspension.
  • This policy operates together with the Terms and Shipping Policy.
  • Mandatory consumer rights under applicable local law remain unaffected.
