Privacy Policy

Genesis Core processes personal data only as needed to operate commerce, secure systems, support customers, and improve platform reliability.

Effective date: February 9, 2026

Last reviewed: February 9, 2026

Data Categories

What We Collect

  • Identity and contact data (name, email, shipping and billing address, phone where provided).
  • Order and payment metadata required to process transactions and prevent fraud.
  • Account activity, support communications, and subscription preferences.
  • Technical data including browser, IP-derived region, device type, and session diagnostics.

Processing Grounds

Why We Process Data

  • Contract performance: order fulfillment, delivery updates, and customer service.
  • Legitimate interests: security monitoring, platform operations, and service optimization.
  • Legal obligations: tax, accounting, fraud prevention, and compliance requests.
  • Consent: marketing communication where required by law.

Data Sharing

Authorized Recipients

  • Payment processors, logistics carriers, and support platforms required for order operations.
  • Infrastructure and analytics vendors operating under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Regulators, courts, or law-enforcement entities where disclosure is legally required.

Genesis Core does not sell personal information.

Retention and Transfers

Storage, Access, and International Flow

  • Data is retained only for legal, operational, and dispute-resolution periods.
  • Cross-border transfers may occur through providers operating in multiple jurisdictions.
  • Where required, transfer safeguards are implemented through contractual and security controls.

Regional Rights

U.S., EEA, UK, and Similar Regimes

  • You may request access, correction, deletion, portability, or restriction of eligible data.
  • You may object to certain processing or withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis.
  • Residents in qualifying jurisdictions may appeal denied requests under local law.

Security and Minors

Safeguards and Age Limits

  • We use technical and organizational controls designed to reduce unauthorized access risk.
  • No internet transmission is guaranteed fully secure; users should protect their own credentials.
  • Services are not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect such data.
